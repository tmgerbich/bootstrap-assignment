About Us

Welcome to Oyster Haven, your go-to spot for the freshest, most delicious oysters! Located by the scenic coast, we proudly source our oysters from local, sustainable farms. Our friendly team is here to help you enjoy oysters, whether you're a seasoned fan or a first-timer. From classic raw oysters to creative seasonal dishes, there's something for everyone. Visit us and savor the taste of the sea at Oyster Haven!

Delivering Quality to Our Community

Trusted business for over 20 years

Oyster Haven began in 2002 when Jeff and Stanley Oyster decided to bring their delicious family tradition of preparing oysters to the larger community. Growing up along the coast, Jeff and Stanley learned the art of oyster shucking and preparation from their grandfather, who passed down secret recipes and techniques that had been cherished for generations. With a passion for sharing their love of oysters, they opened Oyster Haven, turning their family tradition into a beloved local spot. Their dedication to quality and community quickly made Oyster Haven a favorite destination for seafood enthusiasts and newcomers alike.

Locally Sourced

We partner with local fishing operations to ensure that we serve only the freshest and highest quality oysters to you and your family. By collaborating closely with trusted local fishermen, we can guarantee that our oysters are sustainably sourced and harvested at the peak of their flavor. These partnerships not only support local businesses but also allow us to maintain a direct line of quality control, ensuring that every oyster that reaches your plate is as fresh as possible. Our commitment to these relationships reflects our dedication to providing you with the best seafood experience while promoting responsible fishing practices and supporting the local economy.

Giving back

At Oyster Haven, we believe in the power of community and the importance of giving back. Our commitment extends beyond serving the freshest oysters; we're dedicated to making a positive impact locally. We partner with local schools and environmental groups to support marine education and coastal conservation efforts. By hosting beach clean-ups and donating a portion of our proceeds to marine preservation initiatives, we strive to protect the beautiful waters that provide our delicious oysters. Join us in our mission to sustain and enrich our community, one oyster at a time.
